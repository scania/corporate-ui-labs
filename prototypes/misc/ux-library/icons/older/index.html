Scania Icons

The design of the modern and custom-made icon system for communication is based on the Scania symbol, the Scania Sans font family and the product DNA. It has been customised with the goal of unifying the Scania brand experience throughout all applications. The icons enable clear and instantaneous communication for information graphics and sales support. The icons should always be supported by text (except for some UI icons used in web applications). Use the icons for information, never for decoration or as logotypes.

Restrictions: The icons may only be used for their intended purposes. Any restrictions must be followed. The icons may not be modified. Any questions may be submitted to Scania identity helpdesk via identity@scania.com.
