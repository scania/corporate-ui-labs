Photographs

Scania’s image style is set to bring us closer to the customers, the vision and the people. The images are dramatised, using natural light with depth of field and high-level realism. This way Scania is ”present” and shows that the customers and every bit of their business are understood. Two perspecitves may be used to illustrate our awareness of the big picture, as well as our exact attention to detail:
